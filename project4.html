POSTER - Dessert Company

Programs Used

Illustrator

2021

The poster was designed as advertisement for Asomi Mochi, a Japanese dessert company that opens seasonally at the Richmond Night Market. The design was made to capture the essence of a night market while also incorporating personal elements of the client such as their cat.

A challenge that arose from designing such a minimalist poster was creating an interesting art piece that informs the audience that the image takes place at a night market and ensuring that the Japanese dessert is the focal point.

Process

Create line art from sketch and establish the overall scene

Applied base colors from chosen scheme

Add dimension through subtle outlines, shading, and highlights
