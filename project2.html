HARD SURFACE MODEL - Futuristic Gun

Programs Used

Maya

2019

This model was designed as secondary weapon for a futuristic space military. I discovered a fitting illustration from Pinterest, which I referenced to create an interpreted low poly model.

Creating the intricate decorative components of the gun were difficult and required me to use tools I was not yet exceptional at. I practiced creating more difficult shapes than those in the gun design, which provided me exceeding knowledge to complete the decorative components.
