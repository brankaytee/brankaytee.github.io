HARD SURFACE MODEL - Apple AirPods Max

Programs Used

Blender

2021

This 3D model was created while referencing Apple's first wireless headphones. I chose to recreate this product as it had various hard surface shapes which allowed me to apply different modelling techniques. Examples are modelling along a curve for the head band, creating an array of indents for the dial, and rounding edges for the ear cups. Regarding texturing, I applied a cross hatched cloth image that I found online to the ear cushions after itâ€™s UV had been unwrapped. The plastic and shiny metal material were created through blenders material node editor. I created a dramatic lighting to emphasize the metal on the Apple AirPods Max and did a final render.

Difficulty arose when I worked on creating the holes inside the ear cushion as I added too many subdivisions and made the mesh too complicated. Looking back, I should have created the main form of the overall model before detailing the surface. Another issue I faced was manually moving the vertices of the head band to recreate the curve. This technique was tedious, however through research I discovered the technique of moving a mesh along a manipulated curve to get the desired bend.

Process

Reference Image (https://www.apple.com/ca/shop/buy-airpods/airpods-max/sky-blue)

Modelled ear cup and cushion as one mesh using extrustions

Created the dial using incremental inward extrusions around a cylinder

Applied a subtle curve to the mesh for the button on the ear cup

Introuduced a deep curve to create an arc for the headband
