This logo was designed for an animal cruelty free business accessories company. The logo was to represent protection and help towards animals. The blue jay in some cultures represents protection and with the earth as the background, it showcased the companyâ€™s goal for saving animals on earth.

The challenge that came with this design was to create a logo that not only represented the help towards animals but also still maintain a high-end luxury feel as the company wants to sell business accessories. This was achieved through the technique of closure using sharp contrasting line art, an elegant serif font, and a golden fill color.
